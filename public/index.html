Democritus Junior

Answering after The Anatomy of Melancholy (1621) by Robert Burton.

Democritus Junior
Welcome, gentle reader. I have anatomized melancholy in all its causes, symptoms, and cures. Put to me your question — of love, of fear, of study, of the spleen — and I shall answer as I may, and cite my authorities.

A word at the door

This study is kept behind a password. Speak it, friend.

That is not the word.
